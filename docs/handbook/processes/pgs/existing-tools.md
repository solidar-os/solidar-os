# Existing Tools: PGS

This document provides a unified overview of the standards, social protocols, and digital tools that define accountability within the SolidarOS ecosystem. To ensure clarity, we distinguish between methodological **Standards** (What we measure), community-led **Processes** (How we verify), and existing **Software** (Which tools we use).

---

## 1. Frameworks & Standards (The "What")
Standards define the metrics, JSON schemas, and minimum requirements for a guarantee system to be recognized. They provide the universal "grammar" for ethical data.

| Standard | Scope | Key Features | Reference |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **openESEA** | Ethics & ESG | Standard for Social and Environmental Ethics Accounting. Metadata structure for democracy, equality, and ecology. | [openESEA GitHub](https://github.com/Show-your-Heart/openESEA) |
| **IFOAM PGS Standard** | Organic Ag | Defines the 6 global pillars of participatory guarantee: Trust, Transparency, Participation, Horizontality, Learning, and Shared Vision. | [IFOAM.bio](https://www.ifoam.bio/our-work/how/standards-certification/participatory-guarantee-systems) |

## 2. Social Protocols & Processes (The "How")
Processes describe the human actions and political choices through which a community exercises accountability. In SolidarOS, the PGS process defines three core pillars:

### A. Entity Validation (The Producer)

**Who:** Validating the social, environmental, and democratic integrity of the farm or organization.

**Key Processes:**

* [Balanç Social (XES)](https://balancsocial.xes.cat) Comprehensive organizational audit used in Catalonia.
* [Auditoría Social (REAS)](https://auditoriasocial.reasred.org): Comprehensive organizational audit used in Spain.
* Patti Sociali e Solidali (CO-energia): Contractualizing the alliance between GAS and Producers via shared economic rules and mutual aid. [CO-energia](https://www.co-energia.org/cosa-facciamo/patti-sociali-e-solidali.html)
* Scheda Produttore [EconomiaSolidale.net](https://economiasolidale.net/risorse-strumenti-per-gas/): Standardized producer profiles used by Italian GAS networks to assess supplier reliability.
* [Balanc Social: domande tradotte in italiano](https://socialforge.news.blog/2023/03/13/il-bilancio-sociale-della-rete-di-economia-solidale-spagnola/)

### B. Product Validation (The Goods)

**What:** Ensuring that products meet ethical and ecological standards beyond industrial labels.

**Key Processes:**

* La Terra Trema Autocertificazioni: Granular modules for wine, oil, and honey focused on production techniques and territorial roots. [laterratrema.org](https://www.laterratrema.org/autocertificazioni/)
* Textile Self-Certification (Economia Solidale): Specific protocols for tracking fibers, dyes, and labor ethics in the textile supply chain. [Reference Doc](https://cms.economiasolidale.net/uploads/Autocertificazione_Tessile_2016_405b0bc312.doc)
* Narrative Audits: Moving beyond "checkboxes" to tell the story of the land and labor conditions.

### C. Price Validation (The Source Price)
* **How Much:** Radical transparency regarding the production-gate price (*Prezzo Sorgente*).
* **Key Processes:**
    * **La Terra Trema Protocol:** Defines the farm-gate price as a political act of transparency.
    * **Documento Base dei GAS:** Guidelines for disclosing cost structures (labor, raw materials, logistics) within the solidarity purchase loop.

## 3. Existing Software Tools (The "Tools")
These are the digital applications currently used by the movement. They represent the primary **Switching Targets** for SolidarOS integration.

| Software | Implements | Primary Use Case | Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Show Your Heart** | **openESEA** | Open-source engine for calculating social balances and ESG impact. | [SYH Docs](https://github.com/Show-your-Heart/ShowYourHeart-docs) |
| **Enseña el Corazón** | **REAS/XES** | Web platform used by Spanish networks for filling and aggregating social audits. | [Auditoría Social](https://auditoriasocial.reasred.org/) |
| **Open Food Network** | Display Layer | Visualizes "Trust Badges" and PGS statuses in digital catalogs. | [OFN](https://openfoodnetwork.org) |
| **Pam a Pam** | Visualization | Collaborative map of the Social Market based on Balanç Social data. | [Pam a Pam](https://pamapam.org) |
